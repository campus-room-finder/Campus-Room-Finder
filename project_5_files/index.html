ABOUT OUR PROJECT

I love photography

Our project aims to help students find the location of their classroom and provide specific direction to get there. Most of the navigation tools only provide direction for the building without any information for the location of room inside. Other than Tech, most buildings don¡¯t have an interactive application for students to use to locate a room or office but instead perhaps one large map of the building at one location on the main floor. This map may not be easily accessible and may be time-consuming to read, especially with large buildings or buildings of many floors. If this map is not user-friendly or not readily accessible, students may waste time wandering the floors of Northwestern¡¯s buildings in search of a room number, making them potentially late to their classes. With a phone application that has all the building¡¯s room layouts in one place, all a student would have to do is ask the interface where their desired room is, with (ideally) a visual representation shown of where the room is located and perhaps even textual directions of how to get there from every main entrance..
